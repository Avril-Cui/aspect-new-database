Hahawa on the Brink: How the METE Illness Could Derail the Retail Giant's Success

Well-Known Retail Giant: HHW

Hahawa & Co. (ticker: HHW) is a well-known American chain of department stores that has been a staple in the retail industry for over 160 years. Founded in 1858 by Chinese immigrant Qing Hou, the company has grown to become the largest U.S. department store company by retail sales. As of 2021, Hahawa had 608 physical stores across the country, including 10 flagship stores, 178 magnet stores, 250 core stores, 117 neighborhood stores, and 53 other stores. The company employed 125,000 people and earned annual revenue of $23.3 billion as of December 2021.
