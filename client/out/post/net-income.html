Net Income

Key Definition

Net income (abbreviation: NI) is an indicator of a company's profitability. It occurs on the income statement. For investors and business operators, it shows how much revenue is left after subtracting from all the expenses. It is the most popularly used measurement for companies‚Äô profitability as it calculates the ‚Äúabsolute profit‚Äù, which means the final profit after subtracting all the costs that appeared during the business operation. Net Income = Revenue - COGS - SG&A (Operating Expenses) - Tax - Interests - Other Expenses
