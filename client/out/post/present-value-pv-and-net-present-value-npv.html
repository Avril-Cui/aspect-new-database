As we now understand that money today is worth more than money in the future When calculating the present value of an asset back from its future value, we generally call the theoretical opportunity cost of capital, or the ideal ‚Äúinterest rate/return rate‚Äù the discount rate.

The term is also linked to the basic concept of opportunity cost. Opportunity cost is the potential return given by an alternative or similar investment. By using these rates in the calculation of PV, it represents the highest money that the investor should accept.
